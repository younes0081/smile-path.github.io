Hair Transplantation

Hair transplantation is the process of permanently transplanting new hair to the area, needed by people between the ages of 18 and 60 who have experienced hair loss for any reason or who have a genetically wide forehead. This procedure is performed in a hospital environment, under sterile conditions, by doctors and health teams specialized in this field, using microsurgery.
 Hair transplantation in Hair of Istanbul takes place in 4 stages;

Determination of the Hairline

Determining the correct hairline in the hair transplantation process is the most important step for the aesthetic appearance. The hairline should be determined within the natural limits of the person, by evaluating the age and genetic structure of the person, and should be created according to the request of the person by providing a certain amount of symmetry without going beyond the technical rules.
 As Hair of Istanbul, the most important reason why we attach importance to the hairline is to ensure that people are satisfied with this line that they will see for a lifetime.

Beard Transplantation

Beard transplantation can be applied to any individual who has no beard genetically or who has lost his beard for certain reasons, who has completed the age of 20 and who has no health problems that prevent the procedure to be performed with the FUE Technique.
In this process, the basic principles of which are the same as hair transplantation, it is not necessary to shave the entire hairy area.

Hair Transplantation for Women

In the hair transplantation process for women, the appropriateness of the procedure is decided according to the specific situation of the person, taking into account the affected area. If the person is suitable for hair transplantation, the procedure is performed using the FUE Technique. As Hair of Istanbul, we take care to carry out the transplant process without cutting more than 90% of the hair, unlike men, in the procedures performed in women.

Forehead Reduction With Hair Transplantation

Since 2013, as the Hair of Istanbul team, we have performed many forehead reduction procedures. The reason why we consider the forehead reduction procedure as a separate title is that the wishes of people with a genetically wide forehead without hair loss to have a narrower forehead image can be realized with hair transplantation procedure. The most important issue in forehead reduction procedures is to create a new hairline suitable for the muscle structures on the forehead of the person. In forehead reduction procedures, the hair transplanted with the right hairline and the right technique looks mostly natural and the person gets the forehead line and hairline they want.
